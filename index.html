What Does It Do?

Answer five sets of questions about your academics, career goals, finances, behaviors, and life plans — and see an honest projection of your financial life from graduation to retirement.

🎓

College Financial Model

See projected student debt, starting salary, and net worth at retirement based on your real choices — degree field, school tier, scholarships, and behavior.

📊

Lifetime Financial Projection

A year-by-year spreadsheet from your first job to age 65, showing salary growth, taxes, living expenses, savings, and net worth — all in today's dollars.

⚠️

Honest Warnings

The tool flags financially unsustainable paths and suggests alternatives — including vocational training and military service — when the numbers point that way.

What the Model Includes

✓GPA projection based on high school grades and behavior
✓Student debt from tuition, grants, scholarships, and internships
✓Sliding income tax brackets (not a flat rate)
✓Salary growth, career mobility, and advanced degree impact
✓Housing costs, children, and dual-income households
✓Investment growth and cumulative savings to retirement
✓Net worth at 65 shown in today's dollars (inflation-adjusted)
✓P10 / P50 / P90 outcome range with probability distribution chart

About This Tool

Built by an engineer with 20 years of experience hiring and mentoring college graduates. The financial model reflects real hiring practices, career progression patterns, and the financial outcomes that actually play out over a working lifetime — not idealized projections.

The tool is intentionally direct. It shows students the financial consequences of their choices — including drug and alcohol use, poor course planning, and unsustainable debt loads — because those consequences are real, and high school is the right time to understand them.

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

This tool provides projections only. Actual outcomes depend on effort, aptitude, market conditions, and circumstances beyond the model's scope. Use with trusted adult oversight. Not a substitute for professional financial or career counseling.
